Data and privacy

Your lawn record starts on your device.

TurfNote saves your lawn profile, zones, job history, plans, product notes, and timing details locally first. Optional iCloud sync is handled through Apple when it is available on your devices, and scheduled jobs can be exported or imported as JSON files you choose.
